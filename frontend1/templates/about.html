About QuizBot

QuizBot is a smart quiz generator tool built to help educators, students, and businesses create engaging quizzes effortlessly from PDFs and documents.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify assessment creation. Whether you're a teacher preparing exams or a content creator building interactive content, QuizBot automates question generation using AI, helping save time and boost engagement.

How It Works

  • Upload a PDF or document.
  • Let QuizBot generate MCQs and quizzes using smart AI logic.
  • Customize, preview, and share quizzes easily.
